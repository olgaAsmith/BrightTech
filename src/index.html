BrightTech

BrightTech is a multi-platinum selling group, music writer, DJ and record producer. They have written for numerous pop stars including Chloe Roberts, Jacob Smith, Natalie Johnson and Adam Lee. Their catalog has amassed over 1 billion streams worldwide. More than 100 million of those streams can be attributed to their artist career and include such hits as Tyler Nelson's "Echoes," Olivia Martinez's "All I Need" and their own single "Escape." They were also a featured vocalist on David Guetta's #1 Rendezvous album. Their latest music video, "Lost in the Moment," reached 1 million streams in the first week, releasing independently. BrightTech is currently in the studio working on a self-produced album slated for release in 2022.
